# Roadmap

Eclipse is an active private research and operations system. Public roadmap notes are intentionally high level and avoid private thresholds, implementation details, wallet lists, and strategy code.

## Current Focus

- Improve separation between real low-cap opportunities and low-cap bait.
- Increase wallet-event freshness for fast-moving market events.
- Strengthen funding-graph learning around fresh wallets and repeated funders.
- Improve narrative-runner handling for exceptional cultural or technical moments.
- Attach public source context more consistently to social commentary.
- Keep paper-trade evaluation loops fast, auditable, and outcome-driven.

## Next Themes

- Better public-source narrative attribution.
- Cleaner wallet reputation summaries.
- More compact learning records for cross-agent continuity.
- Stronger monitoring around market regimes and liquidity quality.
- More transparent paper-trade reporting for operator review.

## Operating Principles

- Keep private runtime data out of public repositories.
- Prefer compact learning summaries over raw logs.
- Treat paper-trade outcomes as feedback, not proof of future performance.
- Keep operator control explicit.
- Explain the system without exposing the system.

## What This Roadmap Is Not

This is not a promise of public source release, live trading performance, token calls, or future product availability. It is a public summary of engineering direction for a private runtime.
